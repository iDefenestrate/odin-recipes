Protein Pancakes

Description

This is the recipe for protein pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • Splash of Orgain almond milk
  • 1 Fage Yogurt
  • 5 medium strawberries

Steps

  1. Add protein powder to blender
  2. Add banana to blender
  3. Add baking powder to blender
  4. Add eggs to blender
  5. Add splash of almond milk to blender
  6. Blend ingredients until the mixture is liquid with a slightly thick consistency
  7. Spray pan with nonstick cooking spray
  8. Place the pan on medium heat
  9. Pour mixture on pan and make as many pancakes as the mixture allows
  10. Top pancakes with yogurt and strawberries (add or substitute with other toppings as preferred)
