Protein Smoothie

Description

This is the recipe for a strawberry banana protein smoothie. It consists of vanilla protein powder and organic almond milk (a lower-calorie substitute to whole milk and another great source of protein). A single banana with around ten strawberries - I prefer them frozen, but fresh strawberries work, too - are blended with the protein powder and almond milk to create a refreshing, protein-rich beverage to enjoy between meals, or immediately before/after your workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 150g strawberries (frozen)
  • 240 mL Orgain Almond Milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla Prolific Isolate All-Natural Whey protein powder

Steps

  1. Add banana to blender
  2. Add strawberries to blender
  3. Pour almond milk into blender
  4. Pour protein powder into blender
  5. Blend the ingredients for 1 minute (or until desired consistency is achieved)
